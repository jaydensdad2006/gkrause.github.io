It is 20 pixels wide by 1410 pixels tall. Scroll down the page to see the entire length of the image.

When set as the page background, this image will tile horizontally to fill the page creating a top-border background with a white content area below. If the page content becomes longer than 1410 pixels, the background will tile horizontally as well and a blue band will be drawn across the bottom of the page.
